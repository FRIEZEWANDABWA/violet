My Story

From humble beginnings to servant leadership

Born to Serve

Born and raised in Mihuu, I know firsthand the struggles of everyday citizens. Before leadership, I was a small-scale trader and tailor, working hard to provide for my family. But I always carried a dream bigger than myself: to serve my people and transform our community.

The Journey Begins

That dream became reality in 2013 when I was nominated MCA. In 2017, you entrusted me with your votes—and again in 2022. Each step has been fueled by your faith and my unwavering commitment to service.

"Leadership is not about titles—it's about service, integrity, and leaving no one behind."

Proven Impact

Tangible results that speak for themselves

Road Networks

Improved road infrastructure across Mihuu Ward, connecting communities and boosting economic activities.

Clean Water Access

Expanded access to clean water through strategic borehole projects, serving over 1000 families.

Healthcare Facilities

Construction and upgrade of dispensaries for better healthcare access across the ward.

Education Support

Fair and transparent bursary distribution, supporting over 10 schools and hundreds of students.

Land Rights

Strong voice in defending community land and heritage, including the protection of Chetambe Hills.

Youth Programs

Football tournaments, mentorship programs, and skills training for youth empowerment.

Vision 2027 & Beyond

Taking Mihuu's voice to Parliament

Leadership doesn't stop at the ward level. My mission is to take Mihuu's voice to Parliament, where bigger policies and larger resources can transform our lives. As your next MP, I envision a constituency where every child gets an education, every youth has a job or skill, and every family enjoys better healthcare and infrastructure.
