My Story

From humble beginnings to servant leadership

Born to Serve

Born and raised in Mihuu, I know firsthand the struggles of everyday citizens. Before leadership, I was a small-scale trader and tailor, working hard to provide for my family. But I always carried a dream bigger than myself: to serve my people and transform our community.

Growing up in a modest family, I learned the values of hard work, integrity, and community service from an early age. These values have shaped my approach to leadership and continue to guide my decisions as your MCA.

The Journey Begins

That dream became reality in 2013 when I was nominated MCA under ODM. It was a humbling experience to be trusted with representing the voices of Mihuu Ward. In 2017, you entrusted me with your votes—and again in 2022 under FORD-Kenya.

Each step has been fueled by your faith and my unwavering commitment to service. Every project completed, every family helped, every child supported through bursaries - these are the milestones that define my journey.

Leadership Philosophy

My leadership is rooted in the belief that true service means being accessible, accountable, and action-oriented. I don't just make promises during campaign seasons - I work tirelessly every day to improve the lives of our people.

Whether it's attending community barazas, visiting project sites, or meeting with families in need, I believe in hands-on leadership that delivers tangible results.

"Leadership is not about titles—it's about service, integrity, and leaving no one behind."
