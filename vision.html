Vision 2027 & Beyond

Taking Mihuu's voice to Parliament

Leadership doesn't stop at the ward level. My mission is to take Mihuu's voice to Parliament, where bigger policies and larger resources can transform our lives. As your next MP, I envision a constituency where every child gets an education, every youth has a job or skill, and every family enjoys better healthcare and infrastructure.

My 5-Point Agenda for Parliament

Comprehensive development strategy for our constituency

01

Education First

More bursaries, scholarships, and digital learning hubs for our children's future.

  • • Increase bursary allocation by 300%
  • • Establish digital learning centers in every sub-location
  • • Partner with universities for local campuses
  • • Vocational training programs for practical skills
02

Youth Empowerment

Vocational training, sports development, and start-up funding for our youth.

  • • Youth enterprise fund with low-interest loans
  • • Modern sports facilities and equipment
  • • ICT training and digital literacy programs
  • • Mentorship programs with successful entrepreneurs
03

Healthcare for All

Modern facilities and expanded dispensaries across every village.

  • • Upgrade Mihuu Health Center to Level 4 hospital
  • • Mobile clinics for remote areas
  • • Specialist medical camps quarterly
  • • Health insurance support for vulnerable families
04

Agriculture & Jobs

Support for farmers, market access, and investment in agribusiness.

  • • Modern farming techniques and equipment
  • • Cooperative societies strengthening
  • • Direct market linkages and value addition
  • • Agribusiness incubation centers
05

Infrastructure Development

Roads, water, and electricity to every corner of our constituency.

  • • Tarmac roads to all major centers
  • • Reliable electricity connection for all
  • • Clean water in every household
  • • Internet connectivity and digital infrastructure

Implementation Timeline

Strategic rollout of development initiatives

Year 1

Foundation & Planning

Establish parliamentary office, conduct comprehensive constituency needs assessment, and begin priority infrastructure projects.

Year 2

Education & Youth Focus

Launch expanded bursary program, establish digital learning centers, and begin youth enterprise fund operations.

Year 3

Healthcare Expansion

Upgrade health facilities, launch mobile clinic services, and implement health insurance support programs.

Year 4

Agriculture & Infrastructure

Complete major road projects, establish agribusiness centers, and ensure universal electricity access.

Year 5

Consolidation & Growth

Evaluate progress, expand successful programs, and prepare for continued development beyond 2027.
