FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

We typically respond to service requests within 2-4 hours during business hours. For emergency support, we offer 24/7 response with guaranteed response times based on your service level agreement.

Do you provide free consultations?

Yes, we offer free initial consultations to understand your requirements and provide recommendations. This includes site visits for larger projects and detailed assessment of your current I.T infrastructure.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Nairobi and surrounding areas in Kenya. For projects outside this region, we can discuss availability and may partner with local technicians to ensure proper support.

Do you offer maintenance contracts?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance contracts that include regular system checks, preventive maintenance, priority support, and discounted rates on additional services.

What is your warranty policy?

We provide comprehensive warranties on all our installations. Hardware typically comes with manufacturer warranties, while our installation work is covered by our service guarantee. Specific terms vary by project type.
